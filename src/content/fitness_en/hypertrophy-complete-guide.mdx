Muscle hypertrophy is driven by three mechanistic pillars: **mechanical tension**, **metabolic stress** and **muscle damage**. Mechanical tension is the dominant factor.

## Optimal weekly volume

Current evidence converges on 10–20 effective sets per muscle group per week.
